Our Venue

This is an organized competitive community centered around the fighting game genre of video games; bringing in players from all around the region to learn, compete, collaborate and focus on improving the play of the overall community in all relevant games. Now that the FGC is part of the eSports landscape, there are many exciting opportunities and events popping up all over the area and beyond. We look forward to continuing to grow and establish a proud winning tradition and brand that will be recognized throughout the entire competitive gaming landscape!

We welcome players of ALL skill levels, from completely new to the genre ~ to hardened tournament veterans, and everyone gets equal access to train, share and excel as a group.

Currently we hold weekly sessions at Buffalo Game Space Wednesday nights from 7pm-12am for Weekly Ranking Tournaments, and on Saturdays for open practice and casuals from 12pm-1am.

We are a diverse community and almost every fighting game that has been released at one time has graced the setups of the BFGC. You will find players for almost anything here, especially current tournament games being the most popular.
